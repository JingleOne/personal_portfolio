The rule of columns

Every time that is no moving blocks in the screen, three blocks will spawn from the top of the screen and come vertically stacked. Blocks comes in different colors and the amount can be fixed in the program easily for harder game play. When more than three blocks with the same color are aligned vertically, horizontally, or diagonally, the blocks will be eliminated from the screen together.
