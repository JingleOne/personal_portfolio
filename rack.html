DIY Powerlifting Rack

Built a sturdy powerlifting rack and workout station in my backyard

The motivation behind this project

Covid-19 Pandemic Lockdown

When the public health authority administered the lockdown order, public gathering, restaurants, and gyms were closed. My favorite extracurricular activity is weightlifting, and due to Covid-19, I was forced to stay at home. I became unmotivated without the normal social life and weightlifting. Therefore, I decided to build this at-home workout station to alleviate this dilemma.
